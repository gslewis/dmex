Long Division

Overview

Long Division produces long division problems of varying difficulty. A long division problem lays out the working that is necessary to reach the answer.

Problem Definition

Currently long division problems are defined by difficulty level. A problem is defined by its divisor and dividend. From these the answer in the form of a quotient and a remainder can be calculated.

The choice of divisor and dividend is given by the difficulty level. The ranges of values are:

DifficultyDivisorDividend
Easiest11->1314->19
Easy14->19101->999
Medium21->39101->999
Hard41->991001->9999
Hardest51->49910001->99999

Hints

If selected, a table of multiplication equations for the divisor is shown with the problem. The multiplier value is from 2 to 9.

Number of Problems

The number of problems is fixed by difficulty level and there is only a risk of duplication at the Easiest level (which has only 18 unique problems).

Answers

Answers, with the quotient, remainder and each stage of working filled in, can be provided on a separate sheet or on the same sheet as the problems in the right-hand margin.
